I was always an artist, drawing ever since I could hold a pencil. Today, I am passionate about the intersection between art, design, and computer science. Currently, I am exploring different ways of expression through my favorite mediums: charcoal, ink pens, and oil paints. I strive to incorporate storytelling and technology into my pieces.

Right now, I am pursuing a BSE in Computer Science/Digital Media Design and a minor in Fine Arts at the University of Pennsylvania. This semester, I am working on improving my 3d modeling skills, studying algorithms, and learning C++ to make computer graphics.
