Hi, I'm Gabby, a current Digital Media Design student at UPenn. I have been drawing ever since I was little and am passionate about the intersection between art, visual culture, and computer science. I am currently pursuing a BSE and a minor in Fine Arts.

I love exploring different ways of expression. My favorite mediums are charcoal, ink pens, and oil paints and my favorite subjects are people and buildings/architecture. As I continue studying art in school and as a hobby, I hope to improve my digital art skills and 3D modeling, as well as incorporate storytelling in my pieces.
